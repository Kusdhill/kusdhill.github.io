1. Generational Differences in Economic Opportunity (1946 to 1996)

2. Objective

The purpose of this visualization is to show how mean wages, adjusted for inflation, differ across generations in the United States. The visualization would allow users to compare annual salaries at early, mid, and senior career points. By overlaying the data, the visualization may provide valuable insight about how wages have changed over the generations and may reveal patterns coinciding with larger economic trends.

3. Data Sources

  • Bureau of Labor Statistics: Employment, Hours, and Earnings, United States, 1909-90
  • Bureau of Labor Statistics: All employees, thousands, total private, seasonally adjusted

4. Visualization Sources

  • Climate Change Over Time
  • Multi-Series Line Chart
  • In class BRICS line chart
